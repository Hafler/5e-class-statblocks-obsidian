 ## Actions

 ***Meteor Swarm (1/day, 9th level spell).*** 1 mile range, 4 40 ft radius spheres, DC 19 DEX save. On a failed save, the target takes 145 (40d6+5) fire damage and 140 (40d6) bludgeoning, or half damage on a success. A creature only takes this damage once if it's in multiple spheres.

 ***Power Word: Stun (1/day, 8th level spell).*** 60 ft range, 1 target. If the target has 150 hit points or fewer, it is stunned. Otherwise, the spell has no effect. The stunned target must make a Constitution saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on a success.

 ***Teleport (1/day, 7th level spell).*** 10 ft range, the Bard and up to 8 willing creatures within range teleport to anywhere on the same plane of existence.

 ***Chain Lightning (1/day, 6th level spell).*** 150 ft range, 4 targets, DC 19 DEX save. On a failed save, the target takes 10d8 lightning damage, or half damage on a success.

 ***Fireball (3/day, 4th level spell).*** 120 ft range, 20 ft radius sphere, DC 19 DEX save. On a failed save, the target takes 33 (8d6+5) fire damage, or half damage on a success.

 ***Twinned Haste (3/day, 3rd level spell, concentration).*** 30 ft range, 2 targets. Each gets +2 AC, double movement, advantage on DEX saves, and an extra attack. The targets are stunned for a turn when the spell wears off.



## Bonus Actions
 ***Firebolt.*** Ranged Spell Attack: +11 to hit, reach 120 ft., one target. Hit: 27 (4d10+5) fire damage.

 ***Misty Step (2/day, 2nd level spell).*** The Sorcerer teleports 30 feet to a location it can see.


## Reactions

 ***Shield (4/day, 1st level spell).*** As a reaction to being targeted with an attack, the Sorcerer may add 5 to its AC until the beginning of its next turn.
