 ***Multiattack.*** The Warlock uses its Repelling Blast once and its Eldritch Blast once.

 ***Repelling Blast.*** Ranged Spell Attack: +7 to hit, reach 120 ft., one target. Hit: 10 (1d10 + 4) force damage and the target is pushed back 10 feet.

 ***Eldritch Blast.*** Ranged Spell Attack: +y to hit, reach 120 ft., one target. Hit: 10 (1d10 + 4) force damage.


 ***Fear (1/day, 3rd level spell, concentration).*** 30 ft cone, DC 15 WIS save. On a failed save, the target is Frightened and must use its action to dash away. Target repeats the save at the end of its turn if it can't see the Warlock, ending the effect on a success.

 ***Hold Person (1/day, 3rd level spell, concentration).*** 60 ft range, 2 targets, DC 15 WIS save. On a failed save, the target is Paralyzed for 1 minute. Target repeats the save at the end of its turn, ending the effect on a success.
