 ***War Caster.*** The Bard has advantage on saves to maintain concentration.


 ## Actions
 ***Multiattack.*** The Bard uses its Inspire ability and casts a spell.

 ***Inspire.*** One friendly creature within 60 feet adds a d12 to their next D20 roll.


 ***Mass Polymorph (1/day, 9th level spell, concentration).*** 120 ft range, up to 10 willing targets of CR8 or greater. Each target gains 136 temp HP, and becomes a Tyrannosaurus Rex until it has no more temp HP.

 ***Power Word: Stun (1/day, 8th level spell).*** 60 ft range, 1 target. If the target has 150 hit points or fewer, it is stunned. Otherwise, the spell has no effect. The stunned target must make a Constitution saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on a success.

 ***Teleport (1/day, 7th level spell).*** 10 ft range, the Bard and up to 8 willing creatures within range teleport to anywhere on the same plane of existence.

 ***Fireball (1/day, 6th level spell).*** 120 ft range, 20 ft radius, DC 18 DEX save. On a failed save, the target takes 11d6 fire damage, or half on a success.

 ***Dominate Person (1/day, 5th level spell, concentration).*** 60 ft range, 4 targets, DC 18 WIS save. On a failed save, the target is Paralyzed for 1 minute. Target repeats the save whenever it takes damage, ending the effect on a success.```
