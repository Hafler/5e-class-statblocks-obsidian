 - **Languages** Common
 - **Challenge** 15 (13,000 XP)
 ___

 ***Fortified Position.*** The Artificer and its allies within 10 feet have half cover.

 ## Actions
 ***Multiattack.*** The Artifcer attacks once with its Firebolt, and twice with its Force Ballista. It may replace its Firebolt attack with another spell.

 ***Firebolt.*** Spell Attack: +11 to hit, reach 120 ft., one target. Hit: 27 (4d10+1d8) fire damage.

 ***Force Ballista.*** Spell Attack: +11 to hit, reach 120 ft., one target. Hit: 14 (3d8) force damage and the target is pushed 5 feet back.

 ***Cone of Cold (1/day, 5th level spell).*** 60 ft cone, DC 19 CON save. On a failed save, the target takes 41 (9d8) cold damage, or 1/2 damage on a success.

 ***Fireball (6/day, 3rd level spell).*** 60 ft range, 20 ft radius sphere, DC 18 DEX save. On a failed save, the target takes 33 (8d6+1d8) fire damage, or 1/2 damage on a success.

 ## Reactions
 ***Shield.*** As a reaction to being targeted with an attack, the Artificer may add 5 to its AC until the beginning of its next turn.

 ***Flash of Genius (5/day).*** As a reaction to seeing an ally within 30 feet make a saving throw, the Artificer can add +5 to it.

 ***Soul of Artifice (6/day).*** As a reaction to dropping to 0 HP, the Artificer may choose to drop to 1 HP instead. The Artificer takes a -1 penalty to saving throws for each time it has used this ability today.```
