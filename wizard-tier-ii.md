***Sculpt Spells.*** The Wizard's spells don't harm its allies.
 ## Actions
 ***Ray of Frost.*** 60 ft range, 1 target, DC 15 CON save. On a failed save, the target takes 2d6 cold damage and has disadvantage on its next attack roll. On a successful save, the target takes 1/2 damage.


 ***Fireball (3/day, 3rd level spell).*** 120 ft range, 20 ft radius sphere, DC 15 DEX save. On a failed save, the target takes 28 (8d6) fire damage, or half damage on a success.

 ***Hold Person (1/day, 2rd level spell, concentration).*** 60 ft range, 1 target, DC 15 WIS save. On a failed save, the target is Paralyzed for 1 minute. One creature has disadvantage on its first save against this spell. The targets repeat the save at the end of their turn, ending the effect on a success.

 ***Invisibility (1/day, 2nd level spell, concentration).*** The Wizard turns invisible for the next hour, or until it casts another spell.

## Bonus Actions
 ***Misty Step (2/day, 2nd level spell).*** The Wizard teleports 30 feet to a location it can see.

## Reactions

 ***Shield (4/day, 1st level spell).*** As a reaction to being targeted with an attack, the Wizard may add 5 to its AC until the beginning of its next turn.```
