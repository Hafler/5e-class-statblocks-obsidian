***Reckless.*** The Barbarian has advantage on all melee weapon attack rolls, and attack rolls against it have advantage.

***Feral Instinct.*** The Barbarian has advantage on initiative checks, and can act on its turn even if it is surprised.

***Relentless Rage.*** When the Barbarian drops to 0 HP, it can make a DC 10 CON save. On a success, it drops to 1 HP instead. Each time it uses this feature, the DC increases by 5.


 ## Actions

 ***Multiattack.*** The Barbarian makes two attacks with its Greataxe. If it reduces a creature to 0 HP with one of these attacks, it may make a third Greataxe attack.

 ***Greataxe, +2.*** Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, one target. Hit: 25 (1d12 + 19) slashing damage. On a critical hit, the Barbarian does 7 additional slashing damage.```
