***Sculpt Spells.*** The Wizard's spells don't harm its allies.

 ## Actions


 ***Disintegrate (1/day, 6th level spell).*** 60 ft range, 1 target, DC 17 DEX save. On a failed save, the target takes 80 (10d6+45) force damage, or half damage on a success. If this damage reduces a creature to 0 HP, they are disintigrated.

 ***Immolation (1/day, 5th level spell, concentration).*** 90 ft range, 1 target, DC 17 DEX save. On a failed save, the target takes 30 (7d6+5) fire damage and the creature begins to burn, or half damage on a success. A burning creature repeats this saving throw at the end of their turn, taking 10 (3d6) fire damage on a failure, or ending the condition on a success. 

 ***Bigby's Hand (1/day, 5th level spell, concentration).*** Melee Spell Attack: +9 to hit, reach 60 ft., one target. Hit: 18 (4d8) force damage. The Wizard may repeat this attack as a bonus action each turn for the next minute.

 ***Dimension Door (1/day, 4th level spell).*** The Sorcerer and up to one willing creature within 5 feet teleport up to 500 ft.

 ***Fireball (5/day, 3rd level spell).*** 120 ft range, 20 ft radius sphere, DC 17 DEX save. On a failed save, the target takes 33 (8d6+5) fire damage, or half damage on a success.




## Reactions

 ***Shield.*** As a reaction to being targeted with an attack, the Wizard may add 5 to its AC until the beginning of its next turn.```
